After collecting some research paper on study and talking 
     to some students it is found that there should be a software 
     that plans the entire schedule for study.
This app basically helps student to plan their entire schedule
     for exam or for completing any tasks within certain amount 
     of time due to which it can be used by anyone. Notes and 
     reminder are some of the features of this app. Furthermore, 
     features are discussed within the ppt.
